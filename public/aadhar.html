How it works !!

Aadhaar State Wise Daily Enrollment Cumulative Report



What is this content ??

UIDAI (Unique IDentification Authority of India), releases Aadhaar enrollment and rejection counts per state per district with a daily frequency. This app collects that csv data , aggregates and presents the collective information of each state and district. States are ordered in decreasing number of aadhaar enrollments over the webpage.
